Debt recovery has continued

The CRA has resumed its work to address taxpayer debt. Tax refunds, benefit and credit payments may be applied to pay outstanding balances. You can check the status of your benefits by logging into your CRA My Account. Contact the CRA if debt repayment causes you financial hardship. Call 1-888-863-8662 for benefit debt or, 1-888-863-8657 for tax return debt. Learn more at Canada.ca/balance-owing.

CRA My Account access

Sign in to your account

You can sign-in (log in) through one of these options:

  • CRA user ID sign-in
  • Interac® Sign-In Partner bank sign-in
  • Provincial partners sign-in
Not sure how to sign in?

You need to sign in with the same username and password every time you use My Account.

  • When you first registered for a My CRA Account, you selected a particular sign-in method.
  • You might have created a new CRA user id and password just for this account, and written it down or saved it in a Password manager.
  • Or when you registered, you may have decided to use your existing financial institution username and password rather than create a new set for CRA.
  • The CRA My Account tied that particular sign-in username and password with your tax and benefit information. Use that same method every time you sign in.

Expired security code

If your CRA security code expires or has not arrived after waiting 14 days, you can contact us by phone to get a new one.

Recover a locked account

Your account will become locked if you enter the wrong information too many times for any of the following:

  • Password
  • One-time passcode
  • Security questions
  • CRA security code

If your account becomes locked, contact us by phone to have your account unlocked.
