My CRA Account for individuals

View and manage your personal income tax and child and family benefits through this secure portal. You can track your refund and payments, get email notifications and more.

Use the sign-in option you registered with:

CRA  Sign in with your CRA user ID and password   Use your online banking (Sign-in Partner)   Sign in with your province
Not sure which sign-in to use?

You need to sign in with the same username and password every time you use My Account.

  • When you first registered for a My CRA Account, you selected a particular sign-in method.
  • You might have created a new CRA user id and password just for this account, and written it down or saved it in a Password manager.
  • Or when you registered, you may have decided to use your existing financial institution username and password rather than create a new set for CRA.
  • The CRA My Account tied that particular sign-in username and password with your tax and benefit information. Use that same method every time you sign in.

Learn more about My Account privacy and security

Some taxpayers may have received an email notification from the CRA. We are encouraging those who don’t yet have a My Account to register for one. With My Account, you can view CRA mail or check to see if you have any uncashed cheques.

To make your account more secure, email addresses in My Account are now required

As of February 7, 2022, all My Account users will need to have an email address on file with the CRA to help protect their accounts from fraudulent activity. If you do not currently have an email address on file, you will be prompted to provide one when you sign in.

The CRA has added multi-factor authentication

The CRA has added multi-factor authentication for all users to help make your CRA sign-in services more secure! To ensure everyone can use it, we’ve introduced a new passcode grid option. Learn more at Multi-factor authentication to access CRA sign-in services.

If your CRA user ID and password have been revoked

Some taxpayers may have received a notification that their CRA user ID and password have been revoked. Visit CRA user ID and password have been revoked for more information.

Try My Account’s new look and feel!

My Account has a new look! Check it out – sign in and select “Try the new My Account BETA”. It features an improved look and feel, and it’s easier to use. To learn more: My Account – What’s new - Canada.ca

With My Account you can:

  • track your refund
  • view or change your return,
  • check your benefit and credit payments
  • view your RRSP limit
  • change your address
  • set up direct deposit
  • receive email notifications of changes
  • and much more

My Account is fast - transactions are processed immediately.

It's convenient at tax time - you can use Auto-fill my return when you file online using certified software.

You can also set up a payment plan through My Account. To make an online payment, go to Make a payment.

If you are using an older browser that does not support the latest security standard, you will not be able to access CRA's secure online services.
